Our Philosophy

At Artisan Living, we believe a home is more than just a structure—it's a reflection of your life, values, and aspirations. Founded in 2005 by visionary architect James Montgomery, our firm has dedicated itself to creating exceptional residences.

Our approach combines time-honored craftsmanship with innovative design solutions, ensuring each home we build is both timeless and uniquely personal.
