Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our process and services

How long does a custom home project typically take?

The timeline for a custom home project varies based on size, complexity, and site conditions. Typically, the design phase takes 3-6 months, and construction can range from 12-24 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

Our design process begins with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and budget. We then develop conceptual designs, refine them through collaboration, create detailed construction documents, and manage the entire construction process to ensure your vision is realized with precision.

Do you work with a fixed budget?

Yes, we work closely with you to establish a realistic budget early in the process and provide regular updates to ensure we stay on track. We're transparent about costs and work to maximize value while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Can you work with existing architectural plans?

Absolutely. We can work with your existing plans or collaborate with your architect. Our expertise ensures that the design is both beautiful and buildable, with attention to structural integrity, material selection, and construction methodologies.
