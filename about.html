Our Philosophy

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by visionary architect James Montgomery, Artisan Living has dedicated itself to creating exceptional residences that harmonize with their environment while providing unparalleled comfort and beauty.

Our approach combines time-honored craftsmanship with innovative design solutions, ensuring each home we build is both timeless and uniquely personal. We collaborate closely with our clients throughout the entire process, transforming their vision into a living reality.

With over 150 custom homes completed across the country, our portfolio showcases our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for creating spaces that inspire and endure for generations.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work and define our approach

Craftsmanship

We believe in the enduring value of quality craftsmanship, using the finest materials and time-honored techniques to create homes of exceptional quality.

Innovation

Collaboration

We view each project as a partnership, working closely with our clients to ensure their vision is realized with precision and personal attention.
